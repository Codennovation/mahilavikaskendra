About Us

Mahila Vikas Kendra was founded in 1996 by a group of well-educated and successful women who wanted to uplift the social and financial status of the women in society. Our focus has been to build employable skills of less-educated and under-privilege women so that they can find gainful employment to sustain themselves with dignity.

Our Featured Courses

MVK being a non-government, self-help Organization in the field of women empowerment is empowering women through the following.

Counseling.

Since last ten years we are running Family counseling center. We give legal, social, emotional help to the women who are victims of family violence and atrocities. We help the women who are in distress.

Know More 2,928 students enrolled

Training & Education.

We run training center for distress and dropouts of the education system to make them employable. Women are trained in their traditional skills like catering, sewing, beautician etc. and even in the modern skills like computer.

Know More 7,202 students enrolled

Building Entrepreneurship Opportunities.

We encourage entrepreneurship among the women of the weaker section of society. Under this we have catering and tailoring departments.

Know More 12,582 students enrolled

Micro-finance.

We encourage small savings and provide need based loans for the un-banked section of women. Since last 6 years we run credit society and Bachat Gat for this purpose.
